📦 Small Business Backup & Recovery Setup

Standard Value: $150 → Launch Price: $75

When disaster strikes, most small teams discover too late that their backups don't actually work. A working backup isn't just a tech task — it's the core of Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery (BCDR).

I'll give you the most crucial slice of BCDR: a tested, automated backup system you can trust, plus a simple one-page recovery guide that anyone on your team can follow.

  • File-level backup setup — cloud-to-cloud (e.g. Google Drive to OneDrive or Backblaze) or to existing local storage (external drive or NAS provided by client)
  • Automation configured using built-in OS tools or free third-party software
  • Test restore to verify backup integrity
  • Optional: local VM snapshot setup (e.g. VirtualBox, VMware Workstation, EC2 AMIs)
  • One-page recovery guide (plain language instructions to restore files or VM state)

Scope:

  • For individuals or teams (up to 10 devices/endpoints)
  • File and folder backups, or local VM snapshots (no full system image or automated disaster recovery)

Not included:

  • Enterprise backup systems or disaster recovery planning
  • Complex or enterprise virtualization platforms (e.g. VMware ESXi, vSphere, or clustered multi-host environments)
  • Custom scripting or automation pipelines for cloud snapshots

Client needs to provide:

  • Admin access to all devices and VMs to be backed up
  • Configured destination for backups (external drive, NAS, or cloud account)
  • Stable internet connection for cloud backups

👉 This isn't a full enterprise BCDR strategy. It's the affordable first step: knowing your files and projects are safe, recoverable, and won't vanish overnight.

🔐 AWS IAM Foundation Setup

Standard Value: $150 → Launch Price: $75

Every AWS account starts with Identity & Access Management (IAM). Done right, IAM is the foundation that keeps your team secure. Done wrong, it's the weak point that hackers, misconfigurations, and billing confusion exploit.

I follow AWS IAM best practices to build a clean foundation. That means:

  • MFA enforced for all users.
  • Roles and groups that follow least-privilege principles.
  • Billing separation — only finance (and authorized leadership) get billing rights, so charges don't spiral out of control or get exposed to the wrong hands.
  • Simple guide so you can safely add new users later without creating a mess.

Includes:

  • IAM structure (users, groups, roles) using best-practice AWS-managed policies
  • MFA required for all users, billing access isolated
  • Password and access key policies configured

Deliverable:

  • User-friendly guide with IAM overview and step-by-step instructions to add new users safely

Scope:

  • One AWS account
  • Up to 10 users
  • For new or clean accounts only (no active workloads or custom IAM policies)

Not included:

  • Multi-account AWS Organization setup
  • SSO or federated access configuration
  • Custom service-specific IAM policies (available as add-on at $10 per policy)

Client needs to provide:

  • Temporary admin access or screenshare session
  • List of users with names, roles, and what services they need access to (e.g., EC2, Billing, S3)

👉 I don't claim to cover every AWS service. Instead, I secure the essential foundation: the root account, IAM policies, and billing isolation — so your account is safe, organized, and ready to grow.

Flat Launch Price: $75 (Regular $150)
