About Me & The Hexterika Journey

Welcome to my professional space! I'm Sangsongthong Chantaranothai, an aspiring cybersecurity specialist and a versatile IT professional driven by a passion for continuous learning and problem-solving. My journey into technology has been a blend of formal training, diverse real-world experience, and a deep-seated curiosity to understand how things work—and how to secure them.

This page is where I share the broader context of my career, my personal growth, and the philosophy behind Hexterika.

My Path to Cybersecurity & IT

My career in technology isn't just a job; it's a dynamic evolution fueled by a desire to make an impact. From formal roles as an **IT Technician at the Synchrotron Light Research Institute** and an **IT Support Assistant in the Thai Parliament** to my ongoing role as a **trusted IT Generalist for a multi-generational property**, I've consistently embraced technical challenges. These experiences have honed my ability to diagnose complex issues, manage diverse systems (from Wi-Fi networks and IoT devices to enterprise software), and provide clear, patient support to users with varying technical proficiencies.

My pivot towards cybersecurity is a natural progression of this problem-solving drive. I'm fascinated by the intricacies of digital defense and offense, constantly engaging with Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges, hands-on labs, and formal certifications to build a robust skill set ready for immediate application in vulnerability assessment and penetration testing.

The Spirit of Hexterika: Breaking Free & Building

Hexterika is more than just a name; it embodies my commitment to breaking free from conventional career paths and leveraging my diverse skill set to create value. It represents my entrepreneurial spirit and a belief that a "jack-of-all-trades" can indeed master many things, especially when driven by passion and a thirst for continuous improvement.

While **Hexterika Cyberlab** (my dedicated IT & Cybersecurity portfolio) is my primary focus, Hexterika as a whole encompasses a broader range of professional skills. This includes a keen interest in **Figma UX/UI Design** and **social media marketing**, which I continuously hone. These complementary abilities empower me to approach problems with a more holistic perspective, understand user needs, and effectively communicate technical solutions.

My long-term vision for Hexterika is to build a platform where these diverse talents converge, offering unique and comprehensive services. It's a journey of continuous learning, adaptation, and proactive problem-solving, all aimed at creating meaningful impact.
