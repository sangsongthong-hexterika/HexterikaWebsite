Vulnerability Assessment

Vulnerability Assessment

Find verified security weaknesses in your public-facing systems before attackers do.

Best for: Small businesses with websites or public-facing services Starts at: $120

Good fit if

  • You have one public-facing website.
  • You have up to five public IP addresses.
  • You want verified findings, not a raw scanner dump.

What's included

  • Assessment of one website or up to five public IPs.
  • Automated vulnerability scanning.
  • Manual verification to reduce false positives.
  • Severity ranking for verified findings.

Not included

  • Penetration testing or active exploitation.
  • Internal network assessment.
  • Denial-of-service testing.
  • Fixing vulnerabilities for you.

What you receive

  • Vulnerability Assessment Report.
  • Verified findings ranked by severity.
  • Remediation recommendations.
  • Scope and assessment-window summary.

Pricing
