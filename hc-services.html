AWS IAM Foundation

AWS IAM Foundation Setup

Build a secure AWS access foundation before your account becomes messy or risky.

Best for: New or clean AWS accounts Starts at: $90

Good fit if

  • You are starting with a new AWS account.
  • Your AWS account exists, but nothing important is running yet.
  • You want secure access and billing protection from day one.
  • You want guidance without giving away root credentials.

What's included

  • Guidance while you create or prepare your own AWS account.
  • Root MFA setup guidance and verification.
  • IAM users, groups, and roles structured around least privilege.
  • Billing access separated for the right people only.
  • Billing alerts configured to help prevent surprise costs.
  • Password and access key policies configured.

Not included

  • Existing AWS accounts with running workloads or messy permissions.
  • Multi-account / AWS Organizations setup.
  • SSO or federated access.
  • Server, database, storage, or application setup.
  • AWS workload backup or disaster recovery.
  • Full cloud architecture review.

What you receive

  • Secure AWS IAM foundation configured in your account.
  • Environment-specific setup documentation.
  • Plain-language guide for safely adding users later.
  • Billing alert confirmation.
  • Documentation that temporary access was removed after delivery.
  • One included security check-up about 60-90 days after delivery.

How this service works

You own the AWS account and keep root access. I never take your root credentials. You create a temporary admin user for setup, then remove that access after delivery. For the included check-up, you can later create temporary read-only access and remove it again after the review.

Pricing

$150 Launch price: $90

Starting price for a new or clean AWS account. Existing accounts with active workloads or existing access issues belong under AWS IAM Review & Hardening, which is not offered yet.

Backup & Recovery

Backup & Recovery Foundation

Build a reliable, tested backup so your business can recover when something goes wrong.

Best for: Businesses protecting important files Starts at: $90

Good fit if

  • You want reliable backups before disaster strikes.
  • Your files are stored on a computer, cloud service, or AWS.
  • You want confidence that your backups can actually be restored.
  • You want a simple recovery guide your team can follow.

What's included

  • Assessment of where your business files currently live.
  • Backup configured for your environment:
    • Computer → Cloud storage or your own drive.
    • Cloud storage → Independent second backup.
    • AWS → AWS-native backup services.
  • Test restore to verify the backup works.
  • Plain-language recovery guide.

Not included

  • Ongoing monitoring or backup management.
  • Supplying or installing storage hardware.
  • Hosting your backups on my own infrastructure.
  • Enterprise disaster recovery planning.
  • Full system imaging.

What you receive

  • Working backup configuration.
  • Successful restore verification.
  • Recovery guide.
  • Documentation showing what was configured.

How this service works

You keep ownership of your data and storage. I configure the backup using your chosen cloud provider or storage device. If your business has regional or compliance requirements, I'll configure the backup destination accordingly.

Cloud storage charges are billed directly by your chosen provider. My pricing covers the setup service only.

Pricing

$150 Launch Price: $90

Starting price for a standard backup setup. Larger or more complex environments are quoted separately.

How I Work

Client Process

How I Work

A simple, transparent process with clear scope, written approval, and documented delivery.

Step 1 · Free screening chat

We discuss your situation and determine whether one of my services fits. If not, I'll tell you honestly rather than sell you the wrong service.

Step 2 · Agree the scope

I prepare a clear scope document describing exactly what is included and what isn't. We can revise it as many times as needed before work begins, and you can walk away at no cost during this stage.

Step 3 · Written approval

No work begins until I receive your written authorization. Depending on the service, this is either an email confirmation or a signed agreement.

Step 4 · Service delivery

We work using the method best suited to the engagement: guided screen sharing, temporary access, or scheduled remote work. I'll explain the approach before starting.

Step 5 · Delivery & payment

You receive the completed work together with service-specific documentation and evidence of what was delivered. Payment is due after delivery.
