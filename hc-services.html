Vulnerability Assessment

Identify verified security weaknesses in your internet-facing systems before attackers do.

For: Small businesses with public-facing websites or services

From: $120

What's Included

  • Assessment of one external-facing website or up to five public IP addresses.
  • Industry-standard automated vulnerability scanning.
  • Manual verification to reduce false positives.
  • Risk prioritization based on verified findings.

What's Not Included

  • Penetration testing or active exploitation.
  • Internal network assessments.
  • Denial-of-service testing.
  • Social engineering.
  • Remediation implementation.
  • Continuous monitoring.

What You'll Receive

  • Vulnerability Assessment Report.
  • Prioritized verified findings.
  • Severity ratings.
  • Practical remediation recommendations.

How This Service Works

  • Assessment is performed only after written authorization.
  • Only verified findings are included in the final report.
  • The assessment reflects your systems during the agreed assessment window.
